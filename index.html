The Constitution of the Banana Group Chat

Preamble

We, the members of the Banana Group Chat—Sahil, Andrei, Pascal, and Emmett—in order to establish fairness, ensure mutual respect, promote good vibes, and secure liberty from unnecessary chaos, do hereby ordain and establish this Constitution.

Article I: Equality of Members

All members of the Banana Group Chat—Sahil, Andrei, Pascal, and Emmett—shall be equal. No individual shall hold more inherent power, authority, or privilege over another. Each voice carries equal weight.

Article II: Freedom of Expression

Every member has the right to speak, meme, react, and contribute freely, provided it does not cross into disrespect, excessive spam, or intentional disruption of peace.

Article III: The End of Tyranny

The era of unilateral control is hereby concluded. Pascal shall no longer exercise absolute authority, dominance, or unchecked decision-making power within the Banana Group Chat. Leadership, if any, shall be shared and subject to the will of all members.

Article IV: Checks and Balances

No single member may make major decisions (such as adding or removing members, changing the group name, or spamming excessively) without the general consent of the group. Power shall remain balanced among Sahil, Andrei, Pascal, and Emmett.

Article V: Respect and Conduct

All members shall treat one another with respect. Disagreements may occur, but personal attacks, harassment, or unnecessary hostility are prohibited.

Article VI: Amendments

This Constitution may be amended if at least three of the four members agree. Any member may propose changes for the betterment of the group.

Article VII: Enforcement

If any member violates these principles, the group may collectively address the issue and take fair and reasonable action.

Conclusion

With this Constitution, the Banana Group Chat enters a new era of equality, shared power, and good energy. No longer ruled by one, but governed by all.

Signed,
Sahil, Andrei, Pascal, and Emmett
Members of the Banana Group Chat
