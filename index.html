The Constitution of the Banana Group Chat

Preamble

We, the members of the Banana Group Chat—Sahil, Andrei, Pascal, and Emmett—in order to establish fairness, ensure mutual respect, promote good vibes, and secure liberty from unnecessary chaos, do hereby establish this Constitution.

Article I

All members are equal.

Article II

Freedom of expression is allowed unless disruptive.

Article III

No more tyranny. Power is shared.

Article IV

Major decisions require group agreement.

Article V

Respect is required.

Article VI

Changes require 3/4 agreement.

Article VII

Rules are enforced collectively.

Conclusion

No longer ruled by one, but governed by all.
