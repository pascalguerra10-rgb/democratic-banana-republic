Background and History:

The Democratic Banana Republic was not always a democracy. In its early days, it was ruled under Pascal’s original regime, a chaotic and highly questionable fascist-style government that left little room for fairness or input from others. Over time, dissatisfaction grew among the members, particularly Andrei and Emmett, who recognized the need for change. Refusing to accept the status quo, they took action—organizing, challenging authority, and ultimately overthrowing the old system.

In the aftermath, rather than removing Pascal entirely, they created a democratic system and gave him a chance to prove himself again. Meanwhile, Sahil stayed back and watched.
